Cheesecake Recipe

Tarte Portion

Ingredients Quantity
Butter 60g
Egg Yolk 1
Sugar 40g
Bride of Flour 120g
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Put in food processor
  3. In a cake pan, press the mix to create a crust
  4. Bake at 350 F for 10 min
Filling Portion

Filling Portion

Ingredients Quantity
Heavy Cream 1 cup
Eggs 2
Cream Cheese 1 pack
Flour 3 TBSP
Sugar 2/3 cup
Lemon Juice 1 TBSP
  1. Put in food processor
  2. Pour onto crust
  3. Bake at 350 F for about 30 min. Time will depend on oven strength
